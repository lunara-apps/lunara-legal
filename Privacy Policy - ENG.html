Privacy Policy – Daily Mantra

Last updated: 05/05/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Daily Mantra collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the app functionality:

a) Account Information

  • Anonymous user ID (generated by Firebase)
  • Optional login data (Google account or email, if you choose to link your account)

b) User Content

  • Your personal mantra text
  • App progress data (streak, tokens, sessions)

c) Device & Usage Data

  • Basic app usage (e.g. session completion)
  • Device type, OS version (via Firebase Analytics, if enabled)

d) Advertising Data

  • We use Google AdMob to display ads
  • AdMob may collect data for personalized or non-personalized ads

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Save your progress across sessions/devices
  • Improve user experience
  • Display advertisements (AdMob)

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Google Cloud), located in the European Union.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process data based on:

  • Your consent (ads personalization)
  • Contractual necessity (app functionality)
  • Legitimate interest (basic analytics, app improvement)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

We only share data with:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database)
  • Google AdMob (ads)

6. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Withdraw consent (ads)

You can delete your account anytime from the app settings.

7. Data Retention

  • Data is kept until you delete your account
  • Deleting your account permanently removes your data

8. Children

This app is not intended for users under 13 years old.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security (Firebase) to protect your data.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Users will be notified via the app.
