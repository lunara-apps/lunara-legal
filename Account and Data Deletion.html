Daily Mantra — Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how to delete your Daily Mantra account and what happens to the data associated with it.

About Daily Mantra

Daily Mantra is developed by Lunara Apps.

How to delete your account in the app

You can delete your Daily Mantra account directly inside the app:

  1. Open Daily Mantra.
  2. Go to You / Settings.
  3. Open the account or legal section.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Follow the confirmation and re-authentication steps.

Data deleted with your account

When you delete your account, Daily Mantra deletes the data associated with your account, including:

  • Your app account
  • Your saved mantra
  • Your streak and progress
  • Your garden and inventory data
  • Your reminder and settings sync data
  • Your cloud-synced Daily Mantra data associated with your account

Some data may be retained only when required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or abuse-prevention reasons.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email.

Contact email lunara.apps+mantra@gmail.com
Request deletion

Please contact us from the email address associated with your Daily Mantra account, if possible. This helps us verify the request and process it correctly.
