Privacy Policy – Asterveil Tarot

Last updated: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Asterveil Tarot collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. Asterveil Tarot is provided by Lunara Apps.

Asterveil Tarot is a tarot reflection, journaling, and entertainment app. It is designed for symbolic self-reflection and does not provide medical, psychological, legal, financial, or professional advice.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide and improve the app functionality.

a) Account Information

  • Anonymous user ID generated by Firebase Authentication
  • Optional login data, such as Google account or email address, if you choose to link your account
  • Basic authentication metadata needed to keep your account working securely

b) Tarot, Journal, and App Content

  • Daily card data
  • One-card and three-card reading history
  • Selected cards, spread type, reading date, and saved reading details
  • Journal notes or reflections you choose to write
  • Card library and collection-related app data, where applicable

c) App Progress and Settings

  • Lunar Tokens balance and earning/spending history needed for app functionality
  • Unlocked and equipped cosmetic items, such as card backs, reading tables, reveal animations, and card effects
  • Language, reveal style, reduced-motion preference, reminder preferences, and other app settings

d) Device, Diagnostics, and Usage Data

  • Basic app usage events, such as feature usage or reading completion
  • Device type, operating system version, app version, and crash information
  • Analytics and diagnostics data collected through Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, where enabled

e) Advertising Data

  • The app may use Google AdMob to display ads if advertising features are enabled
  • AdMob may collect data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your consent and regional requirements
  • Advertising consent is handled separately from acceptance of the app Terms and Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the app functionality
  • Show your daily card, readings, card library, history, and journal notes
  • Save your progress, settings, Lunar Tokens, and cosmetic unlocks
  • Support account linking and optional cloud sync across devices
  • Send or schedule local reminders, if you enable them
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience
  • Display advertisements if advertising features are enabled and allowed by your consent settings
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the app from misuse

3. Data Storage

Asterveil Tarot is local-first. Some app data may be stored on your device to allow the app to work offline.

If you use account linking or cloud sync, your data may also be stored using Firebase services (Google Cloud), including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics.

Where possible, cloud data is stored and processed using Google Firebase / Google Cloud infrastructure located in the European Union or otherwise handled according to applicable data protection rules.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Where the General Data Protection Regulation applies, we process data based on:

  • Contractual necessity: to provide the app features you request, such as readings, journal storage, account access, and sync
  • Consent: for personalized advertising, optional ad consent choices, and other consent-based features where required
  • Legitimate interest: for basic analytics, crash reporting, app security, fraud prevention, and app improvement
  • Legal obligation: where processing is required to comply with applicable laws

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We only share data with service providers needed to operate the app, such as:

  • Google Firebase, for authentication, database/cloud sync, analytics, and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob, for advertising if ads are enabled
  • Google Sign-In, if you choose to use Google login

These providers process data according to their own privacy and security terms.

6. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation and other applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, such as personalized advertising
  • Request a copy of your data, where applicable

You can delete your account and related cloud data from the app settings where this feature is available. You may also contact us using the email address below.

7. Data Retention

  • Local app data remains on your device until you delete it, reset the app, delete the app, or delete your account.
  • Cloud data linked to your account is kept until you delete your account or request deletion, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
  • Analytics, crash, and advertising data may be retained by third-party providers according to their own retention policies.

8. Children

Asterveil Tarot is not intended for users under 13 years old.

If you are under the minimum age required in your country to use digital services without parental consent, you should use the app only with consent from a parent or legal guardian.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures, including Firebase security features, to help protect your data.

However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device and account credentials safe.

10. Offline Use and Device Storage

Because Asterveil Tarot is local-first, some readings, journal notes, settings, and progress may exist only on your device until sync is available or completed.

If you delete the app, clear app data, lose your device, or use the app without linking an account, some local data may be lost and may not be recoverable.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When required, users may be notified through the app or asked to review and accept updated privacy terms.
