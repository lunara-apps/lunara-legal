Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Mantra Helper collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: 05/05/2026

We aim to collect only the information needed to provide the app experience, save your progress, and support optional features such as account linking and ads.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the app functionality:

a) Account Information

  • Anonymous user ID, generated by Firebase
  • Optional login data, such as Google account or email, if you choose to link your account

b) User Content

  • Your personal mantra text
  • App progress data, such as streak, tokens, and sessions

c) Device & Usage Data

  • Basic app usage, such as session completion
  • Device type and OS version, via Firebase Analytics, if enabled

d) Advertising Data

  • We use Google AdMob to display ads
  • AdMob may collect data for personalized or non-personalized ads

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Save your progress across sessions and devices
  • Improve the user experience
  • Display advertisements through AdMob

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services, part of Google Cloud, located in the European Union.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process data based on:

  • Your consent, for ads personalization
  • Contractual necessity, for app functionality
  • Legitimate interest, for basic analytics and app improvement

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

We only share data with:

  • Google Firebase, for authentication and database services
  • Google AdMob, for ads

6. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Withdraw consent for ads

You can delete your account anytime from the app settings.

7. Data Retention

  • Data is kept until you delete your account
  • Deleting your account permanently removes your data

8. Children

This app is not intended for users under 13 years old.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security through Firebase to protect your data.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Users will be notified via the app.
